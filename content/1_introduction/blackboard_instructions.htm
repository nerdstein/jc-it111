Logging onto BlackBoard

In your browser address window, type ‘blackboard.juniata.edu’ or just ‘blackboard’

  

Login Sequence

Your Login Name is your Network Username (the same login you use on all campus computers)

Your initial Password is ‘juniata’

  

To change your password, go to Personal Information, then Change Password


Finding Your Course

  

Go to the Courses tab, then to Course Catalog

  

Scroll through the list, find your course, then choose Enroll (on the right)

On the next page, confirm your enrollment.
